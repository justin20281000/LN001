About Us

Welcome to Dinner Menu Recommender, your go-to solution for the eternal question: "What should I eat today?"

Our Mission

We understand that choosing a meal can be surprisingly stressful in our busy lives. Decision fatigue is real! Our mission is to add a little fun and spontaneity to your dining choices while introducing you to delicious options from Korean and international cuisines.

How It Works

Our algorithm randomly selects a menu item from a curated list of popular dishes. We provide not just the name, but a visual representation to whet your appetite. Whether you're craving spicy Kimchi Stew or a savory Burger, we've got a suggestion for you.

Contact

We love hearing from our users! If you have menu suggestions or feedback, please use the contact form on our home page.
